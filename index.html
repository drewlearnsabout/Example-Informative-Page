Voting in America

Did you know...

...that if there was a candidate that was accounted for "no-show" votes back in 2016, that candidate would've won over both Donald Trump and Hillary Clinton?

Below are some other interesting facts about voting in the US

The New York Politic's Data Center told the New York Times...

...more than 80% of college-educated Americans turn out to vote compared to 40% that do not hold high school degrees

In recent times...

...60% of eligible voters vote in presidential election years while 40% vote during the midterm elections

Although 24 million Millenials voted in 2016's election...

...it's projected less compared to 2012's elections.
